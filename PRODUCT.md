# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Style-conscious individuals (roughly 20–40) who accumulate clothing but struggle to use what they own. They're at home in the morning deciding what to wear, conscious of weather, and aware that they over-buy and under-wear. They want to feel intentional about how they dress without spending time on it.

## Product Purpose

StyleSync AI digitizes a user's wardrobe using AI image analysis, tracks what they actually wear, and serves daily outfit suggestions calibrated to live weather data. Success: users open the app each morning instead of staring at a full closet feeling like they have nothing to wear.

## Brand Personality

Measured, deliberate, and editorial. The brand speaks in full sentences, not bullet points. It assumes the user is thoughtful, not impulsive. Three-word personality: precise, editorial, unhurried.

## Anti-references

- Fast-fashion apps (Depop, ASOS) — loud, trend-chasing, cluttered
- AI-generated SaaS landing pages with big metric grids (∞ Outfit Combinations, 24/7 Weather Aware)
- Pinterest mood-board aesthetics — collage, saturated, informal
- Cream-palette editorial sameness: every "luxury" app on a warm off-white background with italic serif headlines

## Design Principles

1. Earned minimalism — remove what doesn't earn its place, but never mistake absence for design
2. Precision over decoration — every element exists for a reason; ornament follows function
3. Show, don't explain — the experience communicates the product's intelligence, not the copy
4. Editorial confidence — the brand makes choices; it doesn't hedge with muted palettes and safe layouts
5. Intention over impulse — the product helps users slow down and dress deliberately; the design should feel the same way

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support reduced motion preferences. Body text must meet 4.5:1 contrast on all backgrounds.
